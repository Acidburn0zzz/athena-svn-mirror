tmviewA DVI previewer for SVGA displays.
An SVGA-lib based DVI-previewer offering xdvi-like anti-aliasing, text-string searching, arbitrary-zooming, bookmarks, some hypertex features, and rendering of eps-graphics by invoking ghostscript. It supports The GNU/Linux framebuffer device and double-page viewing. This package was implemented by Thomas Moor and catalogued for CTAN.
