a4Support for A4 paper sizes.
Provides support for A4 paper sizes, however it is mostly superseded by the a4paper option of LaTeX and by the geometry package. It does, however also define the extra option of widemargins. This package was implemented by Nico Poppelier and Johannes Braams and catalogued for CTAN. See also geometry a4wide
