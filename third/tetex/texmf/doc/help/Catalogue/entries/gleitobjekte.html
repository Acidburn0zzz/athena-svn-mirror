gleitobjekteTutorial from a DANTE meeting in November 1997.
The script of a tutorial held at the November 1997 DANTE meeting. Topics covered include floats and their placement, captions, inclusion of graphics, lettering of graphics, layout of tables and large amounts of numerical data. This package was implemented by Axel Reichert and catalogued for CTAN.
