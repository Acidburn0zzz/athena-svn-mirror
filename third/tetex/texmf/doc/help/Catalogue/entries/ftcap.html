ftcapAllows \caption at the beginning of a table-environment.
For several reasons a \caption may be desirable at the top of a table-environment. This package changes the table-environment such that \abovecaptionskip and \belowcaptionskip are swapped. ftcap should also work with your own non-standard table-environment. This package was implemented by Hans Friedrich Steffani and catalogued for CTAN.
