ncctoolsA collection of general packages for LaTeX
The NCCTOOLS bundle contains many packages for general use under LaTeX. Includes tools for: cropmark, watermark, dynamic counter, change counter numbering with other counter, improvements to the description environment, hyphenation of compound words, new levels of footnotes, poor Black Board Bold symbols, alignment of a box, improvements to fancy headers, manual marks in footnotes, extension of amsmath, envelop to the graphicx, improvements to declarations of sections, captions, and toc-entries, improvements in generation of new theorem-like environments, control the text layout. This package was implemented by Alexander I Rozhenko and catalogued for CTAN.
