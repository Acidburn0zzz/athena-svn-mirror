eccSources for the European Concrete fonts.
The MetaFont sources and tfm files of the European Concrete Fonts. This is the EC implementation of Knuth's Concrete fonts, including also the corresponding text companion fonts. This package was implemented by Walter Schmidt and catalogued for CTAN. See also ccfonts
