genmpageGeneralization of LaTeX's minipages.
The GenMPage package generalizes LaTeX's minipages. Keyval options and styles can be used to determine their appearance in an easy and consistent way. Includes options for paragraph indentation and vertical alignment with respect to the visual top and bottom margins. This package was implemented by Thomas Lotz and catalogued for CTAN.
