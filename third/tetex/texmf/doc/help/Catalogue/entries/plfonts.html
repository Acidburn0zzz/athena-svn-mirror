plfontsPolish extension to CM fonts.
Polish extension of Computer Modern fonts. These fonts are compatible with CM fonts; only Polish characters (as in EC/Cork encoding) and quotes are added. The fonts are distributed in METAFONT sources and in Type1 format. This package was implemented by Multiple authors and catalogued for CTAN. See also pl-mf plpsfont
