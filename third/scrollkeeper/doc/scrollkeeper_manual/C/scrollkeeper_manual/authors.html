Authors

ScrollKeeper was written by Laszlo Kovacs (<laszlo.kovacs@Sun.COM>). It would not be possible without contributions from Ali Abdin, Eric Bischoff, Jonathan Blandford, Christopher Gabriel, Paul Jones, Gregory Leblanc, Dan Mueth, and all the people in the MetaLab project who created the OMF.

For more information about ScrollKeeper, visit the ScrollKeeper home page. Please send all comments, suggestions, and bug reports for ScrollKeeper to the ScrollKeeper-devel mailing list.

This manual was written by Dan Mueth (<d-mueth@uchicago.edu>). Please send all comments and suggestions regarding this manual to the author or to the ScrollKeeper mailing list at <scrollkeeper-devel@lists.sourceforge.net>.
