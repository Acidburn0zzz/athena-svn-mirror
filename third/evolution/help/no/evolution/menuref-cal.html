The Calendar View Menu

The Calendar's View menu contains the following items:

Show Shortcut Bar

Toggle the Shortcut Bar on and off with this item.

Show Folder Bar

Toggle the folder bar on and off with this item. See the section called Folders and The Folder Bar in Chapter 1 for more information about the folder bar.

View By Day

Switch to the day view for your calendar.

View Five Days

View five calendar days at once.

View by Week

Switch the calendar view to full week mode.

View By Month

Look at a month at a time.

The Calendar Settings Menu

This menu is empty.
