Subscription Management

Evolution lets you handle your IMAP and newsgroup subscriptions with the same tool: the subscriptions manager.

  1. In the Store section, click on the folder to which you wish to subscribe.

  2. Click Subscribe to add it to the subscribed list.

  3. Close the window.
