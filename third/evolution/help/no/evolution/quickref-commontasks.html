Quick Reference for Common Tasks

You might want to copy this section and tape it to the wall next to your computer: it's a very short summary of most of the things you'll want to do with Evolution. Read if you would like a complete list of keyboard shortcuts for menu commands.

Opening or Creating Anything

Create a new folder

File->New+ + + + + + + + + + + + + + + + + ->Folder or - - - -Ctrl- - - - - -Shift - - - - - -F- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Create a new Shortcut in the Evolution Bar

File+ + + + + ->New+ + + + + ->Evolution Bar Shortcut+ or - - - -Ctrl- - - - - -Shift- - - - - -S- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Create a new email message:

Use File->New->Mail Message+ + or - - - -Ctrl- - - - - -Shift - - - - - -M- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -

Create a new Appointment

File+ + + + + ->New+ + + + + ->Appointment+ or - - - -Ctrl- - - - - -Shift- - - - - -A- - - - - - - - - - - - - - - - -
