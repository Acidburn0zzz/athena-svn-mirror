Chapter 7. Synchronizing Evolution with Hand-Held Devices

This chapter covers is how to synchronize data installed and configured. If you need information on how to set up a synchronization system, consult Chapter 10.

Using HotSync

Put your hand-held device on its cradle and press the HotSync button.

If your environment is correctly configured to sync with your PDA, then it should sync with Evolution.
