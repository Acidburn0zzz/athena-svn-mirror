I'm running S.u.S.E. Linux, how do I get RPM packages?

There is an excellent GNOME for SuSE page at http://gnome.linuxbe.org(Belgium) and http://www.tu-harburg.de/skf/Pub/ifmpc118.ifm.uni-hamburg.de/gnome.html(Germany). They currently have instructions and RPMs for SuSE v6.0, with v5.3 RPMs coming soon. Check it out for more information.
